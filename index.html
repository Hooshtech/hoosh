Welcome to the ultimate solution for all your building-related project needs.

Our flagship product, the Hoosh Tech Portal, is designed to streamline the entire process.
Whether you're a project owner, architect, engineer, contractor, or jurisdiction professional, our portal simplifies submitting applications for plan checks and permit issuance. Enjoy a seamless, efficient, and comprehensive experience—built to save you time and effort.

Our Key Features

Property Analysis

The Property Analysis helps you quickly determine what can be built on a parcel by analyzing zoning, planning constraints, and buildable potential based on local regulations.

AI Code Assistant

Allows users to ask detailed, jurisdiction-aware code questions in natural language and receive instant, context-aware answers across zoning, structural, accessibility, MEP, energy, and more.

AI Compliance Checks

Automatically analyzes uploaded plan files for compliance with zoning, grading & drainage, architectural, structural, MEP, fire & life safety, egress & occupant load, accessibility, and energy & green building codes, generating detailed correction reports and visual markup overlays.

Permit Prep Services

Prepares your application with zoning reports, license verification, jurisdiction-specific requirements, fee estimates, document checklists, and pre-submittal validation—all before you even apply.

Smart Jurisdiction Detection

Automatically identifies the project’s jurisdiction based on the project address and pulls applicable zoning data, restrictions, and local code frameworks instantly.

Guided Permit Application Workflow

Walks users step-by-step through the entire application process—from defining the project scope and uploading plans to validating team licenses and ensuring submission readiness.

Easily Create Permit Applications

Allows users to effortlessly start new permit applications for a wide range of building-related project types.

Upload Plans and Documents

Let’s users upload original or revised architectural plans and all required supporting documents in combination of IFC, PDF, JPG, and PNG formats, with automatic validation and AI re-analysis for each upload—ensuring a complete and compliant submission package.

Revision & Recheck System

Supports up to three rounds of plan updates per project with automatic AI rechecks and feedback improvement, helping applicants achieve compliance faster with fewer delays.

Dynamic Invoicing & Point System

Uses a transparent, point-based billing model where every feature usage is tracked, invoiced, and payable via PayPal—with support for autopay and subscription management.

Multi-User Team Management

Enables company or agency admins to add, manage, and assign user roles and permissions, track verification status, and monitor usage across teams.

Help & Standards Center

Provides an integrated How It Works, FAQs, and Submission Standards section to help users understand the portal's functionality and jurisdictional expectations.

FAQs

How do I sign up for the Hoosh Tech Portal?

To sign up, simply click on the 'Sign Up' menu item and follow the steps. You'll receive a confirmation email after signing up.

+

What can I view on the dashboard of the portal?

The dashboard gives you an overview of all your permit applications, including their current statuses, submission dates, and compliance check progress.

+

Can I delete a draft permit application in the portal?

Yes, any unsubmitted draft can be deleted directly from the dashboard.

+

How do I resume a partially completed permit application in the portal?

Simply click on the saved draft listed on your dashboard to pick up where you left off.

+

Will I be able to view the compliance report from the dashboard?

Yes, once generated, the compliance report will appear as a link within your dashboard.

+

Can I sort or filter applications in the portal dashboard?

Yes, you can filter applications by their status and sort them by submission or update date.

+

Will the portal notify me when a compliance check is finished?

Yes, you’ll receive an email notification and see an update in your dashboard once the compliance check is complete.

Can multiple users in the same company work on a shared application?

Yes, if users belong to the same organization, they can collaborate on shared applications depending on their assigned roles.

What can the upcoming Property Analysis tool in the portal help me with?

It will help users understand build potential by providing:

  • Zoning designation and code interpretations
  • Height limits, setbacks, FAR, and lot coverage
  • Buildable area and potential unit count
  • Highest and best use of the property
+

Will the Property Analysis tool calculate buildable area automatically?

Yes, it will compute buildable square footage, floor area ratio (FAR), and number of dwelling units.

Is zoning data in Property Analysis pulled from real jurisdiction databases?

Yes, zoning information is retrieved directly from official jurisdiction zoning systems.

  • Lot size and dimensions
  • Permitted land uses
  • Development restrictions
  • Overlay zones or constraints
  • And more

  • Energy and green building codes
