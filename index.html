Welcome to Hoosh Tech LLC—your one-stop solution for all building-related projects.

Our flagship product, the Hoosh Tech Portal, integrates advanced AI with intuitive tools to streamline the entire permitting and compliance journey.
Whether you're a project owner, architect, engineer, contractor, or jurisdiction professional, our portal enables you to analyze properties, generate zoning reports, verify licenses, get real-time code answers, perform AI-powered compliance checks, and prepare and submit permit applications for a wide range of building-related project types—all in one centralized platform.
Work faster, smarter, and with greater confidence—saving time, cutting costs, and accelerating approvals.

Our Key Features

Property Analysis

The Property Analysis helps you quickly determine what can be built on a parcel by analyzing zoning, planning constraints, and buildable potential based on local regulations.

AI Code Assistant

Allows users to ask detailed, jurisdiction-aware code questions in natural language and receive instant, context-aware answers across zoning, structural, accessibility, MEP, energy, and more.

AI Compliance Checks

Automatically analyzes uploaded plan files for compliance with zoning, grading & drainage, architectural, structural, MEP, fire & life safety, egress & occupant load, accessibility, and energy & green building codes, generating detailed correction reports and visual markup overlays.

Permit Prep Services

Prepares your application with zoning reports, license verification, jurisdiction-specific requirements, fee estimates, document checklists, and pre-submittal validation—all before you even apply.

Smart Jurisdiction Detection

Automatically identifies the project’s jurisdiction based on the project address and pulls applicable zoning data, restrictions, and local code frameworks instantly.

Guided Permit Application Workflow

Walks users step-by-step through the entire application process—from defining the project scope and uploading plans to validating team licenses and ensuring submission readiness.

Easily Create Permit Applications

Allows users to effortlessly start new permit applications for a wide range of building-related project types.

Upload Plans and Documents

Let’s users upload original or revised architectural plans and all required supporting documents in combination of IFC, PDF, JPG, and PNG formats, with automatic validation and AI re-analysis for each upload—ensuring a complete and compliant submission package.

Revision & Recheck System

Supports up to three rounds of plan updates per project with automatic AI rechecks and feedback improvement, helping applicants achieve compliance faster with fewer delays.

Smart Queuing & Notifications

Users can submit their plan check applications and continue working while our system handles compliance checks in the background. When checks are complete, users are instantly notified by email and through the portal. The system automatically scales to handle varying workloads and plan complexity.

Dynamic Invoicing & Point System

Uses a transparent, point-based billing model where every feature usage is tracked, invoiced, and payable via PayPal—with support for autopay and subscription management.

Multi-User Team Management

Enables company or agency admins to add, manage, and assign user roles and permissions, track verification status, and monitor usage across teams.

Help & Standards Center

Provides an integrated How It Works, FAQs, and Submission Standards section to help users understand the portal's functionality and jurisdictional expectations.
